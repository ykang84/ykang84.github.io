Bio

I have worked as a research assistant in Mid-America Transportation Center at University of Nebraska. Previously, I obtained my master degree of Computer Science at Georgia Tech and finished undergraduate studies in Transportation at Beijing Jiaotong University in Beijing, China.

I am interested in executing data-driven solutions to increase efficiency, accuracy, and utility of data processing. Experienced at statistical analysis, using predictive data modeling, and analyzing data mining algorithms to deliver insights and implement action-oriented solutions to crucial real-life problems.
